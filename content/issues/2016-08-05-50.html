Innate Immunity and Asthma Risk in Amish and Hutterite Farm Children // NEJM

OA | By probing the differences between two farming communities, researchers found that substances in the house dust from Amish, but not Hutterite, homes is associated with changes to immune cells that appear to protect children from developing asthma.
