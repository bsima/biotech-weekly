Google Expands Further Into Biotech, Hardware With Lift Labs Acquisition // Forbes

The search company expanded further into its hardware and biotech realms Wednesday when it said it was acquiring Lift Labs, a small company that has built a high-tech spoon that stabilizes food for people with essential tremor or Parkinson’s disease.

Tissue Engineering

GM embryos 'essential', says report // BBC News

A Hinxton Group report says editing the genetic code of early stage embryos is of "tremendous value" to research, declaring it "essential" that the genetic modification of human embryos is allowed.

The US refuses to fund research involving the gene editing of embryos.

Smart cells teach neurons damaged by Parkinson's to heal themselves // DeepStuff

Genetically modified macrophages produce glial cell-derived neurotrophic factor, or GDNF, and deliver it to the brain to provide support and protection for nerve cells throughout the brain and body. GDNF can also heal and stimulate the growth of damaged neurons.
